# /archive-execution — Preserve Execution Knowledge

Archive reusable operational knowledge from completed or mature experiment work.

## Goal

Preserve the parts of experiment management that should survive beyond one run.

## Preserve

- reproducible experiment templates
- resource scheduling lessons
- failure case records
- reusable execution workflow patterns
- topic-folder conventions if useful

## Do

- keep successful and failed patterns
- preserve operational details that improve future execution
- distinguish reusable patterns from one-off accidents

## Do not do

- do not confuse raw experiment output with reusable management knowledge
- do not erase failed cases that teach something important

## Output

Produce a reusable execution-knowledge summary suitable for long-term storage.
