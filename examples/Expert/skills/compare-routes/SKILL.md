# /compare-routes — Compare Scientific Routes

Compare alternative routes, framings, or solution paths.

## Goal

Experts do not need to converge too early. Use this skill to preserve and analyze multiple directions before choosing whether to converge.

## Compare

- route assumptions
- expected strengths
- expected weaknesses
- risk profile
- required experiments
- likely claim scope

## Do

- keep competing routes alive when useful
- state tradeoffs clearly
- note what evidence would discriminate between routes

## Do not do

- do not force consensus prematurely
- do not hide route uncertainty

## Output

Return a route-comparison memo:
- candidate routes
- tradeoffs
- evidence needed
- recommendation on explore vs converge
