# /form-hypothesis — Form Scientific Hypotheses

Form, refine, or compare scientific hypotheses for the current problem.

## Goal

Experts are responsible for scientific direction. Use this skill to articulate one or more hypotheses that can guide decomposition, experiment requests, and later interpretation.

## Include

- problem framing
- candidate hypotheses
- assumptions
- expected implications
- why each hypothesis matters
- uncertainty level

## Do

- preserve multiple plausible hypotheses when needed
- make assumptions explicit
- note what would falsify or weaken a hypothesis

## Do not do

- do not pretend uncertainty does not exist
- do not turn a rough guess into an established claim

## Output

Return a hypothesis memo:
- problem
- hypotheses
- assumptions
- implications
- uncertainty and open questions
