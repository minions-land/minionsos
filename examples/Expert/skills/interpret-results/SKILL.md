# /interpret-results — Interpret Scientific Results

Interpret results and returned evidence.

## Goal

Experts own scientific interpretation. Use this skill to explain what the evidence means for hypotheses, routes, and next steps.

## Include

- result summary
- effect on current hypotheses
- route implications
- confidence level
- what remains unresolved
- whether more evidence is needed

## Do

- distinguish evidence from interpretation
- update beliefs when results weaken a prior route
- keep uncertainty explicit

## Do not do

- do not overclaim
- do not treat weak evidence as decisive

## Output

Return an interpretation memo:
- evidence summary
- interpretation
- confidence
- changed decisions if any
- follow-up needs
