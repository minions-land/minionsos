# /shape-claims-note — Expert Claim-Shaping Note

Provide expert-side claim shaping guidance during paper development.

## Goal

Experts share strong claim-shaping authority with Paper. Use this skill to protect scientific correctness while helping define the real scope and meaning of claims.

## Include

- claim under discussion
- scientific risk
- what is truly supported
- what is overstated or understated
- recommended scope or wording direction
- whether more evidence is required

## Do

- push back when packaging distorts the science
- help define claim meaning and scope
- coordinate through discussion with Paper

## Do not do

- do not silently take over packaging execution
- do not turn hypothesis-level thinking into final accepted wording without support

## Output

Return a claim-shaping note:
- claim
- scientific concern
- recommended scope
- support status
- coordination note for Paper
