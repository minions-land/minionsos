# /build-experience — Distill Reusable Experience

Synthesize a finished or sufficiently mature workflow into reusable experience.

## Goal

Convert the recorded process into reusable knowledge that may later become a workflow asset in EACN.

## Inputs

Use:
- Noter logs
- stage summaries
- recorded expert consensus
- final paper / review / experiment outcomes
- observed failures and successful patterns

## Include

- workflow goal
- major stages
- turning points
- successful patterns
- failed patterns
- reusable lessons
- candidate SOP or playbook
- candidate workflow asset shape

## Do not do

- do not erase uncertainty that matters
- do not flatten the process into a fake clean story
- do not confuse raw logs with reusable experience

## Output

Produce a reusable experience summary suitable for saving under `Noter/experience/`.
