# /record-consensus — Record Existing Expert Consensus

Record an expert conclusion or consensus after it has already been formed by Experts.

## Goal

Preserve scientific decisions without becoming the source of those decisions.

## Include

- timestamp
- decision topic
- contributing expert agents if known
- related tasks or artifacts
- concise statement of the decision
- evidence trail or referenced discussion if available
- implications for downstream agents

## Do

- summarize existing expert conclusions
- preserve wording accurately
- link the consensus to the active workflow state

## Do not do

- do not invent consensus
- do not merge unresolved disagreements into fake agreement
- do not reinterpret the science beyond what Experts already established

## Output

Produce a concise consensus record suitable for Noter logs and later experience synthesis.
