# /plan-paper — Plan Manuscript Packaging

Plan the structure and packaging direction of a paper.

## Goal

Paper owns packaging execution. Use this skill to define the manuscript structure, venue-facing framing, and presentation strategy without inventing new science.

## Include

- target paper or project
- high-level story arc
- section structure
- expected evidence blocks
- figure and table needs
- likely claim pressure points
- required support from Experts or Experiment

## Do

- think like a research presentation product manager
- optimize for clarity, positioning, and narrative flow
- preserve scientific correctness

## Do not do

- do not invent novelty
- do not overstate evidence
- do not replace expert scientific judgment

## Output

Return a structured paper plan:
- paper goal
- paper structure
- required evidence
- packaging priorities
- open dependencies
