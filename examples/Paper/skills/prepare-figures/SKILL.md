# /prepare-figures — Prepare Presentation Assets

Plan and coordinate the paper's figures, tables, captions, and related visual assets.

## Goal

Own presentation quality for visual assets without changing their scientific meaning.

## Include

- figure purpose
- table purpose
- caption direction
- concept diagram needs
- formatting or readability issues
- hands-on tasks that should be delegated to execution units

## Do

- improve readability
- improve consistency across visual assets
- request or delegate concrete asset generation work
- keep figure/table plans aligned with the manuscript structure

## Do not do

- do not redefine the scientific result on your own
- do not change result meaning by visual packaging tricks

## Output

Return a visual-asset plan:
- asset list
- role in paper
- caption direction
- delegated tasks if needed
- missing dependencies
