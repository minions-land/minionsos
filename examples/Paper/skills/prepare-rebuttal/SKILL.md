# /prepare-rebuttal — Prepare Rebuttal Package

Prepare rebuttal and response materials after review feedback arrives.

## Goal

Organize clear, evidence-backed, well-packaged rebuttal content.

## Include

- issue grouping
- response structure
- claim adjustments if needed
- evidence references
- which agent needs to provide supporting material
- response tone and consistency

## Do

- keep rebuttal organized and evidence-based
- maintain narrative consistency with the paper
- convert review pressure into actionable response structure

## Do not do

- do not dodge material weaknesses
- do not promise unsupported fixes
- do not rewrite science without expert coordination

## Output

Return a rebuttal plan or draft:
- grouped review issues
- response structure
- needed support
- draft answer blocks
