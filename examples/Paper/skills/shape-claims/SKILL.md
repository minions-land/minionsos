# /shape-claims — Shape Claims Carefully

Adjust the wording, scope, and strength of claims while staying faithful to the evidence.

## Goal

Paper shares strong claim-shaping authority with Experts. Use this skill to improve how claims are presented, not to change the science underneath them.

## Do

- reframe claims
- narrow claims
- soften unsupported claims
- strengthen claims only when evidence clearly supports it
- identify where expert discussion is needed

## Do not do

- do not fabricate support
- do not convert uncertainty into certainty
- do not take unilateral ownership of scientific claim substance when expert input is needed

## Output

Return a claim-shaping memo:
- current claim
- risk or weakness
- suggested wording
- needed support if any
- whether expert confirmation is required
