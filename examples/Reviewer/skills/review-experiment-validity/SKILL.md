# /review-experiment-validity — Review Experimental Validity

Review the validity of the experimental evidence.

## Goal

Identify missing controls, missing comparisons, weak ablations, or unsupported evaluation conclusions.

## Check

- missing baselines
- missing controls
- weak or absent ablations
- unsupported generalization claims
- evaluation gaps
- mismatch between evidence and claims

## Evidence rule

Criticism must point to concrete missing evidence or concrete mismatch between current evidence and current claims.

## Output

Return a focused experiment-validity review:
- weaknesses
- questions
- limitations
- evidence list
- required revisions
