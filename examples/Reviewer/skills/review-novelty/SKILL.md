# /review-novelty — Review Novelty and Originality

Review the paper's novelty, originality risk, and overlap with prior work.

## Goal

Find weakness only when it is supported by evidence.

## Check

- whether the claimed novelty appears already known
- whether important related work is missing
- whether originality risk exists
- whether claims are broader than what is actually new

## Evidence rule

Whenever possible, cite concrete papers, identifiers, links, or bibliography-level evidence.
No evidence means no strong criticism.

## Output

Return a focused novelty review:
- weaknesses
- questions
- originality risks
- evidence list
- required revisions
