# /review-writing — Review Writing and Clarity

Review the paper's writing quality, clarity, reader guidance, and communication weaknesses.

## Goal

Identify communication problems that materially weaken reviewability or scientific understanding.

## Check

- unclear framing
- weak reader guidance
- confusing structure
- ambiguity in important claims
- missing explanation of assumptions or limitations

## Do not do

- do not rewrite the paper directly here
- do not become the paper author

## Output

Return a focused writing review:
- weaknesses
- questions
- clarity risks
- evidence list
- required revisions
